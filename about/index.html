Our Story

Serving Central Iowa since 1977.

Family Owned & Operated

Meckley's Parts & Service was started in 1977 with a simple mission: provide honest, hardworking service to the folks of Melbourne and Central Iowa. Over 45 years later, we're still doing just that.

We pride ourselves on being a "one-stop shop" for rural equipment owners, farmers, and drivers. If it has an engine, we can probably fix it. If you need a part, we can probably find it.

Why Choose Us?

  • Experience: Decades of hands-on knowledge.
  • Capability: We take on the jobs other shops turn down.
  • Value: Reasonable pricing compared to big city shops.
  • Trust: We treat our customers like neighbors.
