Welcome to Squareville

The ultimate destination for 1973-1987 Chevy Truck enthusiasts.

A Passion for Squarebodies

We don't just sell parts; we live and breathe these trucks. "Squareville" is our dedicated section for 1973-1987 Chevrolet and GMC C/K series trucks. Whether you're restoring a K10, fixing up a C20 camper special, or building a custom rig, we speak your language.

Hard-to-Find Parts

Our yard has a rotating stock of squarebody donors. We often have:

  • Rust-free body panels (when we can find them!)
  • Interior trim and dashes
  • Small block and big block engines
  • Factory axles and transfer cases
  • Trim pieces and badges
